Deploying a IN link with render

Before starting you need to have a Github Account.

If your account gets suspended, you will have to sign up for Render with a different email.

Temp Gmails - SmailPro

Step 1

Visit latest working repo



Step 2

Go to Render, and make an account
MAKE IT WITH GMAIL (You have to use a gmail, or it will make you verify with payment information.)
Click "New +" and then click "Web Service". Now, click "Build and deploy from a Git repository" and then "Next"
Press 'Public Git repository' and paste the link in the box and press continue
Name the project and then click "Create Web Service" and wait for it to deploy.
